Frequently asked questions

Do my patients need to download any app?
+
No. Patients simply message your clinic's WhatsApp number, the same app they already have. There is nothing to download, sign up for, or learn on their side.
Which WhatsApp do I need?
+
You need a dedicated phone number for the clinic bot, connected through WhatsApp Business API. We guide you through the full setup process; it takes about 10 minutes and we are with you every step of the way.
Can patients book in Hindi?
+
Yes. The bot automatically detects whether the patient is writing in Hindi or English and responds in the same language. We support Hindi and English at launch.
What if I have existing patients in a register?
+
You don't need to import anything. The bot builds patient profiles automatically from the very first booking. Your existing patients simply start using the WhatsApp number from their next appointment.
Is my patient data safe?
+
Yes. All patient data is stored on servers in India, encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share or sell patient data. You own your complete patient list and can export it at any time.
Can I use my existing clinic WhatsApp number?
+
If your existing number is already on WhatsApp Business API, yes. If it's on regular WhatsApp or the free Business app, we support the migration process completely.
What if I want to cancel?
+
Cancel any time directly from your dashboard; no phone calls, no forms, no questions asked. Your data remains available for export for 30 days after cancellation.
Is there a free trial?
+
Yes, 7 days completely free with no credit card required. You get full access to all Growth plan features during your trial.
