Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when you sign up, use our services, or contact us. This includes name, email, phone number, clinic details, and appointment data.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to provide and improve our WhatsApp booking service, send reminders, manage patient records, and communicate with you about your account.

3. Data storage and security

All patient and clinic data is stored on servers in India. We use encryption at rest and in transit. We comply with applicable data protection laws including DPDP.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell or share your patient data with third parties for marketing. We may share data only as required by law or to provide our services (e.g., WhatsApp API).

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, or request deletion of your data. You may export your patient list at any time. Contact us at WhatsApp for requests.

6. Contact

For privacy questions: Message us on WhatsApp or email info@margx.in
